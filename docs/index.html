About STUDORIUM

STUDORIUM partners with schools, registered training organisations, technical and further education providers, universities, and businesses to design and implement innovative, effective, and engaging learning experiences.
We combine design thinking, educational research, and technology integration to enhance both academic and vocational outcomes.

STUDORIUM is the genitive plural of the Latin word studium, which means “study,” “zeal,” or “dedication.”
